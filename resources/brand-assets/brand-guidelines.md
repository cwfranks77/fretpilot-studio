# FretPilot & The Franks Standard - Brand Guidelines

## Color Palette
- FretPilot Primary: #00FFD0 (Aqua)
- FretPilot Dark: #181C24 (Charcoal)
- The Franks Standard Gold: #FFD600
- The Franks Standard Dark: #232323
- Accent: #FFFFFF (White)

## Typography
- Headings: Montserrat Bold
- Body: Montserrat Regular or Arial

## Logo Usage
- Use horizontal logo for website headers
- Use icon-only (circle or square) for profile photos
- Maintain clear space around logo (at least 1x height)
- Never stretch, skew, or recolor logos

## Social Media
- Profile: Use icon-only version (circle for FretPilot, square for The Franks Standard)
- Cover: Use full logo on dark background

## Example Applications
- App icon: FretPilot circle, #00FFD0 background
- Store icon: The Franks Standard square, #FFD600 background
