Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2024

Quick Summary: FretPilot Studio offers a 3-day refund window for all purchases made through the Google Play Store. Refunds require a reason and are processed according to Google's refund policies.

1. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • Your purchase was made within the last 3 days
  • You provide a valid reason for the refund request
  • Your purchase was made through the Google Play Store

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for FretPilot Studio:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your Android device
  2. Tap the Menu (three horizontal lines) in the top-left corner
  3. Select "Account" or "Payments & subscriptions"
  4. Find your FretPilot Studio purchase
  5. Tap "Request a refund" or "Report a problem"
  6. Select a reason from the dropdown menu (required)
  7. Provide any additional details in the text field
  8. Submit your refund request

Alternative Method: You can also request a refund directly from the Play Store website at play.google.com/store/account

3. Required Information

When requesting a refund, you must provide:

  • Reason for refund (required) - Select from the provided options:
    • I didn't mean to purchase this
    • I purchased by accident
    • The app doesn't work as expected
    • I found a better alternative
    • Other (with explanation)
  • Purchase details - Order number and date of purchase
  • Additional comments (optional) - Any additional information that may help us improve

4. Refund Processing Time

Refund requests are processed by Google Play Store. Typical processing times:

  • Automatic approval: Usually within 1-2 business days
  • Manual review: May take up to 5 business days
  • Refund to payment method: 3-10 business days after approval

5. Refund Limitations

The following limitations apply:

  • Refunds are only available within 3 days of purchase
  • Refunds cannot be processed after the 3-day window has expired
  • Refunds are subject to Google Play Store's refund policies
  • If you've used premium features extensively, refund eligibility may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis

6. Google Play Store Refund Policy

FretPilot Studio refunds are processed through Google Play Store. Google has its own refund policy that applies to all purchases:

  • Google may automatically approve refunds within 48 hours
  • After 48 hours, refunds require manual review
  • Google reserves the right to deny refund requests that don't meet their criteria
  • For more information, visit: Google Play Refund Policy

7. Subscription Refunds

For subscription-based purchases:

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time through Google Play Store
  • Refunds for unused subscription periods are available within 3 days of the most recent charge
  • Canceling a subscription stops future charges but does not refund previous payments
  • To cancel: Google Play Store → Subscriptions → FretPilot Studio → Cancel

8. In-App Purchase Refunds

For one-time in-app purchases (e.g., lifetime premium access):

  • Refund requests must be made within 3 days of purchase
  • A reason must be provided
  • Once refunded, premium access will be revoked

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about refunds or need assistance:

Email: support@fretpilotstudio.com

Website: fretpilotstudio.com

Response Time: We typically respond within 24-48 hours

10. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this refund policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of FretPilot Studio after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
