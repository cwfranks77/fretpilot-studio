FretPilot Studio is your intelligent guitar practice companion - guiding daily sessions, highlighting weak spots, and helping you build consistent progress. Instead of guessing what to practice next, you get structured drills, adaptive feedback, and motivating analytics that keep you moving forward.

Key Features
- Smart Practice Trainer: Run focused sessions that adapt to timing, accuracy, and consistency as you play.
- AI Lesson Generator: Get fresh, personalized exercises aligned with your current level and goals (technique, speed, fretboard navigation, rhythm).
- Chord & Scale Library: Browse clean diagrams across the fretboard; quickly compare alternate fingerings and positions.
- Mistake Heatmap: Visual visualization of note accuracy - instantly see patterns to target improvement.
- Metronome & Tuner: Precise tools so timing and intonation are locked in before you start a drill or performance.
- Progress Analytics: Track streaks, session count, tempo growth, accuracy trends, and focus areas.
- Jam Companion: Practice musical feel with backing grooves in multiple styles and tempos.
- Practice Planner: Set micro-goals and auto-sequence drills for efficient daily routines.
- Premium Enhancements (optional): Expanded AI lesson depth, advanced analytics layers, and additional groove packs.

How It Helps
FretPilot Studio reduces friction: open it, choose a goal (speed, tone, chord changes, fretboard mastery), and run a guided block. Immediate feedback and visual tracking show what improved and what still needs attention - keeping motivation high.

Designed For
- Beginners establishing clean fundamentals
- Intermediates accelerating speed and fretboard familiarity
- Self-taught players adding structure
- Returning guitarists rebuilding technique
- Busy musicians needing efficient, targeted sessions

Respect for Your Data
Only practice metrics you enable are analyzed to tailor recommendations. You control collection settings. A clear privacy policy describes what is stored, why, and how it can be cleared.

Getting Started
1. Tune up using the built-in tuner.
2. Pick a focus (e.g., chord transitions).
3. Run a Smart Trainer session; review the heatmap and analytics summary.
4. Generate an AI lesson to reinforce weak spots.
5. Track streaks to build consistent habit loops.

Why Consistency Matters
Small, daily, targeted sessions compound faster than occasional long jams. FretPilot Studio keeps sessions measurable so gains are visible - turning disciplined practice into a rewarding loop.
