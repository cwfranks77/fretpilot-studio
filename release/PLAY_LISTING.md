## Full description

FretPilot Studio helps you play smarter and have more fun on guitar.

- AI Lesson Generator: personalized drills and riffs in seconds
- Trainer: metronome, tuner, and practice heatmaps
- Jam Companion: backing tracks and chord progressions
- Music Store: curated gear with secure checkout
- Progress: streaks, goals, and analytics to stay motivated

Built with fast, offline-ready tech and a clean, distraction-free UI.
