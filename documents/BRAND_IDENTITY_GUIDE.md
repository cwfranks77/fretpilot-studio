## Logo Usage Guidelines

### When to use FretPilot Master Logo:
- App splash screen
- App store listings
- Main website header
- Business cards
- Official communications

### When to use The Franks Standard Logo:
- Store page header
- Product packaging
- Invoices and receipts
- Store-specific marketing
- Physical retail (if applicable)

### When to use Combined Badge:
- Co-branded merchandise
- Social media profile pictures
- App icon (simplified version)
- Dual-branded campaigns
