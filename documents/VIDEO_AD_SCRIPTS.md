## Production Checklist

### For Each Video Ad:

**Pre-Production:**
- [ ] Finalize script (exact word count for timing)
- [ ] Create shot list (every camera angle needed)
- [ ] Scout locations (home studios, clean backdrops)
- [ ] Cast talent (if using real people) OR source stock footage
- [ ] Secure music licensing (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or original)

**Production:**
- [ ] Film all scenes (shoot 3x more footage than needed)
- [ ] Capture b-roll (hands on guitar, close-ups, product shots)
- [ ] Record voiceover (professional mic, quiet space)
- [ ] Screen record app demos (high-res, smooth)

**Post-Production:**
- [ ] Edit timeline (sync to music beats)
- [ ] Color grade (match brand colors)
- [ ] Add motion graphics (logo animations, text overlays)
- [ ] Mix audio (voiceover + music balanced)
- [ ] Export multiple aspect ratios (16:9, 1:1, 9:16, 4:5)
- [ ] Add subtitles/captions (85% watch with sound off)

**Quality Check:**
- [ ] First 3 seconds hook instantly
- [ ] Brand visible within first 5 seconds
- [ ] Clear CTA in last 5 seconds
- [ ] Readable on mobile (text large enough)
- [ ] Complies with platform specs (file size, length)

## Recommended Production Partners

### Freelance Video Editors (Fiverr/Upwork)
- **Budget:** $200-500 per video
- **Turnaround:** 5-7 days
- **Deliverables:** Edited video, multiple formats, revisions included

### Stock Footage Sources
- **Artgrid:** $39/month (unlimited downloads, cinematic quality)
- **Storyblocks:** $30/month (large library, good for b-roll)
- **Pexels:** Free (limited selection, lower quality)

### Music Licensing
- **Epidemic Sound:** $15/month (unlimited tracks, YouTube safe)
- **Artlist:** $16.60/month (high-quality, full license)
- **AudioJungle:** $1-50 per track (one-time purchase)

### Voiceover Artists
- **Voices.com:** $100-500 per script (professional, studio-quality)
- **Fiverr:** $50-200 per script (varies by quality)
- **DIY:** Use your own voice + microphone ($50-150 USB mic)
