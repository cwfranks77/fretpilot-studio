FretPilot Privacy Policy

Last updated:

FretPilot ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app uses and why.

Data We Collect

  • Anonymous analytics (optional): With your consent, the app may send anonymous usage events (e.g., feature usage) to our server to improve the experience. You can toggle consent any time in-app.
  • Advertising IDs (mobile only): The mobile app integrates Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID) and coarse location for ad delivery, per Google policies.
  • Crash/diagnostics: We do not integrate 3rd‑party crash analytics at this time.
  • Account/payment: If you purchase or subscribe, Stripe (and, on Android for eligible digital subscriptions, Google Play Billing) processes payments. We do not store full card details. See Stripe Privacy Policy and Google Privacy Policy.

How We Use Data

  • Operate core features (lessons, practice tracking).
  • Improve app quality and prioritize features.
  • Show non‑personalized or contextual ads (AdMob) where applicable.

Your Choices

  • Consent: Anonymous analytics only runs with your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Ads: Mobile platforms allow resetting the Advertising ID and controlling ad personalization.

Data Retention

Anonymous analytics events, if enabled, are retained only as long as necessary for analysis. No personal profiles are built.

Age Limits & Children's Privacy

FretPilot is intended for users aged 13+ and is not directed to children under 13. Premium, paid, or subscription features require users to be at least 16 years old (or the applicable age of consent to contract in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages. If you believe a child provided personal information, please contact us to delete it.

Content Standards

FretPilot does not provide or permit access to adult content. User‑generated content, where available, must comply with our Terms and content guidelines.
