Welcome to FretPilot Studio ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe user experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

By using FretPilot Studio, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Username or display name (for login and personalization)
  • Email address (optional, for account recovery and communication)
  • Practice data (lessons completed, practice time, progress tracking)
  • User preferences and settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system, app version)
  • Usage statistics (features used, session duration, interaction patterns)
  • Performance data (app crashes, errors, response times)
  • Local storage data (cached lessons, offline content, preferences)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment information directly (handled by app stores)

  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect sensitive personal information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Personalize your learning experience with AI-generated lessons
  • Track and display your practice progress and statistics
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important service updates and notifications (if email provided)
  • Respond to support requests and feedback
  • Detect and prevent technical issues and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Most of your data (username, practice history, preferences) is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage or app-specific storage.
  • Cloud Storage: Currently, we do not sync data to cloud servers. All data remains on your device.
  • Security Measures: We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your data.
  • Data Retention: Your data is retained locally until you clear app data or uninstall the application.

Third-party services operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review:
