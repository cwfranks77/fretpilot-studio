About FretPilot Studio & School

FretPilot is a modern music learning and gear platform combining AI‑assisted lessons, practice tools, and a curated store for instruments and accessories. Our goal is to help musicians learn faster, practice smarter, and gear up with confidence.

What We Offer

  • AI‑powered video lessons and personalized practice plans
  • Metronome, tuner, chord library, and jam tracks
  • Storefront for instruments, DJ and pro‑audio, and accessories

How We Sell

  • FretPilot‑fulfilled: Items we stock and ship directly or via logistics partners.
  • Dropship: Partner/distributor ships directly to you after order.
  • Affiliate: Select items may link to trusted retailers; purchases are completed off‑site.

Content Standards

FretPilot does not provide adult content. We aim to keep learning environments welcoming and safe for all musicians.
