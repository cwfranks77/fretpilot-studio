Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the tuner need microphone permission?

The tuner analyzes live audio to detect pitch. Audio is processed in real-time and never recorded or stored.

How do I reset my progress data?

A reset feature is planned for v1.1. Currently, you can clear app data via Android Settings → Apps → FretPilot Studio & School → Storage → Clear Data.

Is my practice data synced to the cloud?

Not yet. All data is stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is planned for a future update.

How do I enable/disable analytics?

Open the app → Settings → Privacy → Analytics Consent. Toggle on or off as desired.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Core features (tuner, metronome, heatmap, practice analyzer) work fully offline. AI lesson generation may require internet for future expansions.

What devices are supported?

Android 7.0 (API 24) and above. Optimized for phones and tablets.

Known Issues (v1.0.0)

  • Tuner latency on very low frequencies (<60Hz): Workaround: tune higher strings first, then octave-adjust.
  • Heatmap fret offset after rapid tuning: Reopen Mistake Heatmap to refresh.
  • Jam Companion double-trigger slowdown: Manually reset tempo in control panel.
