Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 26, 2025

Overview

The Franks Standard ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit thefranksstandard.com or use FretPilot Studio.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics (such as anonymized Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. This includes page views, approximate location (country/city level), device type, and referral sources.
  • Affiliate Tracking: If you arrive via an affiliate referral link, we store a referral code in a cookie and local storage for 30 days to properly attribute sales and pay commissions. This does not identify you personally.
  • Contact Information: If you email us at hello@thefranksstandard.com, we'll retain your email address and message content to respond to your inquiry.

How We Use Your Information

  • To improve our website and services based on usage patterns
  • To process and fulfill affiliate commissions
  • To respond to your questions and support requests

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) who are contractually obligated to protect your data
  • With affiliate partners to process commissions (only referral codes, not personal data)

Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and browser local storage for:

  • Remembering your affiliate referral code (30-day expiration)
  • Tracking whether you've dismissed our affiliate disclosure banner
  • Analytics (if enabled)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking (use browser privacy extensions or Do Not Track)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Our site uses HTTPS encryption, and we follow industry best practices for secure hosting and data storage.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect changes. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
